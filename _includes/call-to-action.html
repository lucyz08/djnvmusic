About Niko

Niko (now 16) took up DJing at 10 years old. Starting off playing a majority of Top 40 Pop and Greek music, Niko now enjoys listening to Latin Trap, Rap, EDM, as well as a plethora of other genres.

Niko has a diverse set of interests and hobbies. He handles all public relations for Student Government, founded and runs Greek Club, and is now on the board of Best Buddies.

In the past, Niko has provided music for school dances, church events, birthday parties, and weddings. He is very excited to DJ your next event!
